PhenoFusion3D

A project focused on integrating 3D plant phenotyping, imaging, and data-driven analysis to support plant science research and innovation.

Learn More

About the Project

PhenoFusion3D aims to support advanced plant phenotyping by bringing together imaging, analysis, and visualization tools into one platform. This website is the starting point for the project and will continue to grow as students contribute new ideas and improvements.

Core Areas

The project can evolve over time, but these are some of the main areas of focus.

3D Phenotyping

Capture and analyse plant structure using 3D data to better understand growth, morphology, and plant traits.

Imaging Integration

Combine multiple imaging sources such as RGB, multispectral, or hyperspectral data for richer plant analysis.

Data Analysis

Support quantitative analysis through visualisation, measurements, and data-driven workflows.

Student Contributions

Provide a foundation that students can extend with new pages, features, experiments, and improved design.

Project Vision

PhenoFusion3D is intended to become a collaborative platform that helps communicate the goals, progress, and outputs of the project.

  • Showcase the purpose and scope of the project
  • Present research directions and technical progress
  • Provide a place for documentation and future updates
  • Allow students to gradually improve both the content and design
